Privacy Policy (English)

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

1. Data Collection & Usage

  • Local Storage: Your personal data, such as tasks, schedules, and icons, are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store this data on our servers.
  • AI Processing (Google Gemini): To provide AI-powered task insights, certain text data (e.g., task titles) is transmitted to Google Gemini API. This data is used solely for real-time analysis and is not stored by us to identify you.
