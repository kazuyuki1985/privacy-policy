SkinRitual – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kazuyuki Nakamura ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the SkinRitual app.

1. Information We Collect

SkinRitual stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit personally identifiable information to external servers.

  • Skin progress photos: Taken and stored entirely on your device. Photos are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Routine logs & notes: Stored locally on your device only.
  • Location data: Used solely to fetch your local UV index from a public weather API. Your precise location is not stored or transmitted to us.

2. Camera & Photo Library Access

SkinRitual requests access to your camera and photo library to allow you to capture and save skin progress photos. All photos remain on your device and are never shared with third parties.

3. Location Access

SkinRitual requests "When In Use" location access to provide personalized UV index information. Location data is sent to a public weather API (e.g., Open-Meteo) solely to retrieve UV index values. We do not store your location data on any servers.

4. Notifications

SkinRitual may request pe rmission to send local push notifications (e.g., Golden Hour skincare reminders). Notifications are generated on-device and do not involve any external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

SkinRitual may connect to a public weather/UV API to retrieve UV index data. No personal information is shared with this service beyond the coordinates needed to look up the UV index for your area.

6. Data Storage & Retention

All user data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling SkinRitual will permanently delete all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

SkinRitual is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: kazuyuki19850805@gmail.com
