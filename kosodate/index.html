Data Collection & Usage

  • Baby Records (Local Storage Only)
    Baby name, date of birth, weight, height, milk intake, breastfeeding time, temperature, and milestone records are stored only on your device. We do not collect or upload this data to any server.
  • AI Feature – Anthropic Claude API
    When you use the AI advice feature, your baby's growth records (e.g., weight, height, feeding amounts) may be sent to the Anthropic API to generate advice. We do not send personally identifiable information such as names or dates of birth. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data.

Children's Privacy

KosoDate is designed for parents to record their baby's information. We do not knowingly collect personal data from childre n. Baby records entered by parents are stored locally and are not transmitted to our servers.
